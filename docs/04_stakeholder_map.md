# VisitBrief AI — Stakeholder Map

## Document Information

**Project:** VisitBrief AI
**Document:** Stakeholder Map
**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Discovery Phase

---

# Purpose

This document identifies the stakeholders who may influence, use, approve, govern, support, or be affected by VisitBrief AI.

Understanding stakeholder needs is critical because success requires more than user adoption.

The product must also satisfy operational, compliance, security, and governance requirements.

---

# Stakeholder Overview

| Stakeholder          | Role                 | Interest Level | Influence Level |
| -------------------- | -------------------- | -------------- | --------------- |
| Caseworker           | Primary user         | High           | Medium          |
| Supervisor           | Oversight and review | High           | High            |
| Agency Leadership    | Strategic sponsor    | Medium         | High            |
| IT Team              | Technical support    | Medium         | High            |
| Compliance & Privacy | Governance           | High           | High            |
| Product Team         | Product ownership    | High           | High            |
| Citizens / Families  | Indirectly affected  | High           | Low             |

---

# Primary Stakeholder

## Caseworker

### Goals

* Prepare efficiently for visits
* Understand case history
* Identify follow-up items
* Reduce administrative effort

### Concerns

* Inaccurate summaries
* Missing information
* Additional workflow burden

### Success Definition

The worker feels informed and prepared before the visit.

---

# Supervisor

### Goals

* Consistent preparation quality
* Accurate information usage
* Proper case review

### Concerns

* Overreliance on AI
* Missed context
* Unsupported conclusions

### Success Definition

Workers use AI as a preparation tool while maintaining professional judgment.

---

# Agency Leadership

### Goals

* Operational efficiency
* Workforce support
* Consistent processes

### Concerns

* Risk
* Cost
* Adoption challenges

### Success Definition

Preparation efficiency improves without introducing unacceptable risk.

---

# IT Team

### Goals

* Secure deployment
* Reliable performance
* Maintainability

### Concerns

* Security vulnerabilities
* Unauthorized access
* Data leakage

### Success Definition

The system operates securely and reliably.

---

# Compliance and Privacy Teams

### Goals

* Regulatory compliance
* Responsible AI usage
* Appropriate data handling

### Concerns

* Privacy violations
* Hallucinations
* Unexplainable outputs

### Success Definition

The system operates within approved governance boundaries.

---

# Citizens and Families

### Goals

* Fair treatment
* Accurate information handling
* Human review of decisions

### Concerns

* Automated decisions
* Inaccurate information
* Lack of accountability

### Success Definition

Human professionals remain responsible for decisions.

---

# Stakeholder Alignment Principles

VisitBrief AI should:

* Improve preparation efficiency.
* Increase information visibility.
* Preserve human accountability.
* Provide source traceability.
* Avoid decision automation.
* Support responsible AI practices.

---

# Key Discovery Insight

The product must satisfy more than user needs.

It must simultaneously support:

* Worker effectiveness
* Supervisory oversight
* Compliance requirements
* Security expectations
* Public trust

These stakeholder needs will influence future product decisions, requirements, architecture, and AI guardrails.
