# VisitBrief AI Demo Script

## 30 Second Version

VisitBrief AI is a responsible AI assistant designed to help caseworkers prepare for client visits.

The application reviews case records, surfaces follow-up items, identifies deadlines, and generates visit preparation briefs using retrieval-based AI.

The system is designed to support preparation workflows while maintaining human review and accountability.

---

# 3 Minute Demo

## Introduction

Today I'll demonstrate VisitBrief AI, an AI-assisted case preparation workspace.

The goal of the product is to reduce the time required for visit preparation while improving visibility into records, deadlines, and follow-up actions.

The system uses synthetic case data and is intended only as a preparation support tool.

---

## Dashboard

Starting on the dashboard, we can see:

* Case status
* Priority level
* Open tasks
* Record count
* Preparation progress

This provides a quick understanding of the case before beginning a review.

---

## Case Records

Next, I'll open the Case Records view.

This area contains source documents such as:

* Intake summaries
* Contact notes
* Provider notes
* Service plans
* Visit notes

Instead of searching through multiple documents manually, users can quickly review relevant information in one workspace.

---

## Tasks

The Tasks page highlights:

* Open follow-up items
* Ownership
* Due dates
* Preparation checklist items

This helps caseworkers understand what still requires attention before a visit.

---

## Calendar

The Calendar page provides visibility into:

* Upcoming deadlines
* Scheduled activities
* Visit planning milestones

This allows preparation activities to be prioritized effectively.

---

## VisitBrief AI Assistant

The AI Assistant can answer questions about the selected case.

Examples include:

* What follow-up items are open?
* Are there upcoming deadlines?
* Summarize this case.
* Are there conflicting records?

The assistant uses retrieval-based reasoning and only answers from available case information.

---

## Visit Brief Generation

The assistant can also generate a visit preparation brief.

The brief summarizes:

* Case background
* Current concerns
* Open actions
* Deadlines
* Suggested preparation areas

This provides a structured overview before a visit.

---

## Responsible AI Design

Several safeguards were intentionally included:

* Human review required
* No autonomous decisions
* Synthetic data only
* Limited scope assistance
* Retrieval-first architecture

The product is designed to support workers rather than replace professional judgment.

---

## Closing

VisitBrief AI demonstrates how responsible AI can improve workflow efficiency while maintaining transparency, accountability, and human oversight.

The project includes product discovery, workflow design, AI architecture, evaluation planning, deployment, and governance considerations.

Thank you for your time.
